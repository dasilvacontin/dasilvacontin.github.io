About the author

David (dasilvacontin) is… difficult to introduce. He is finishing his Computer Science degree, yet he is currently working as Product Designer at Sensa. He has previously worked at Improbable and Toggl as a Software Engineer.

His final degree project involves exploring what boardgames in the future will be like, through the augmentation of an existing boardgame (Formula D) using a projector and a Kinect sensor – it’s a project that combines Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, and Product Design (User Research, Interaction Design, Experience Design, Prototyping, etc).

Other things he does: programmatic art, making and playing videogames, downhill longboarding, organizing and competing in hackathons, contributing to open source
